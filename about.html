About Me


Hi, I’m Carlos Alberto García Ezquerra, a Data Scientist with more than 3 years of experience in data analysis, business intelligence, and machine learning applications. I specialize in building data-driven solutions that transform complex information into clear insights, empowering teams to make smarter decisions.

I hold postgraduate studies in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning from the University of Texas at Austin, and completed a masters program in Artificial Intelligence from the Universidad Autónoma de Querétaro.

I’m passionate about exploring the intersection of AI, data engineering, and business applications — always looking for ways to bridge technical solutions with real-world impact.

If you’d like to collaborate, feel free to reach out — I’m always open to new opportunities where data meets innovation.
